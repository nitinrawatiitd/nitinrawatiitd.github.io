We like growth. Be it the GDP, the revenue of a company of interest, or our salary. It's a direction that gives you a sense of being on the right path, a brighter and better future.

## Why is growth so ingrained into our psyche?
Growth is abundant in life on earth. A seed becomes a sapling, a sapling a plant or tree. A human baby becomes a child, a child an adult. Proliferation of any life seems to be a gift from the nature (maybe except pests and germs - which is a check to uncontrolled growth)

Territorial growth became necessary for the early agrarian societies when their populations started increasing and their mobility became restricted.
