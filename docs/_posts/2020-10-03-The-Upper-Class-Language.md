I think in every society that exists and has existed, not only there has been a socio-economic divide between the rich and the poor that make up that society but also a language divide.

If we take a look at our own society today, knowledge of and command over the English language is taken as a sign of sophistication and intelligence. The rich and affluent speak fluent English. The poor don't or atleast not that well. There are exceptions but it is true that english is seen as a path to upward social mobility.
